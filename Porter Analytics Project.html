RECOMMENDATION AND INSIGHTS¶

Suggested Strategies: 1.Total Sales of the Dunnage Category of the product is way to low which is in negative (-121307.00). So it is cannibalising the sales of other segments. Thus we have to remove it for the betterment of sales. 2. Analysing the retail sales of all the item types, we can conclude that Liquor and Wine category are the biggest contributors to the total retail sales. Suggested strategy would evolve around increasing overall sales of these two category to catch up on the Total Sales of Beer. 3. Analysis suggests that KEGS category has larger warehouse sales and almost nill retail sales. Suggestive strategy would be around promoting KEGS in retail market. 4. STR_SUPPLIES category has no presence in warehouse while it has substantial sales in the retail market. Suggested strategy should be focused on pushing STR_SUPPLIES into the retail space as well. Insights: 1. Retail sales for the year 2018 and 2020 is low compared to the years 2017 and 2019. 2. Total retail sales for 2020 is comparitively low. This can be contributed to the fact that retail sales for the months 2, 4, 6, and 8 is not available. 3. Beer is the major contributor to the Total Sales standing at 70%. 4. Our top supplier is E&J GALLO WINERY with total retail sales of 166170.53.
